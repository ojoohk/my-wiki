# Behavioral and Brain Sciences (BBS)

Peer-reviewed journal publishing "target articles" — substantial theoretical or empirical papers — accompanied by open peer commentary from invited specialists and a response from the original author. Published by Cambridge University Press.

---

## 2018

- [Singh (2018) *BBS*](../references/Singh_2018_BBS.md) — "The Cultural Evolution of Shamanism" — cultural evolutionary theory of why shamanism recurs near-universally; shaman as practitioner who violates folk notions of humanness (trance as drama of strangeness; initiation as transformation) to claim jurisdiction over uncertain outcomes; explains early professionalization; seven conditions for shamanism's dissolution; target article with open peer commentary
