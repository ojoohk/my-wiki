# Evolutionary Human Sciences (EHS)

Open-access journal of Cambridge University Press. Publishes evolutionary research on human behaviour, cognition, culture, and life history from biological and social science perspectives.

---

## 2023

- [André et al. (2023) *EHS*](../references/Andre_etal_2023_EHS.md) — "Cultural Evolution from the Producers' Standpoint" — theoretical paper; argues cultural evolution models should center on producer fitness costs/benefits, not consumer-side reception; applies Hamilton (1964) social evolution framework to classify cultural production (altruistic, selfish, mutualistic, spiteful); symbolic culture as producer extended phenotype
