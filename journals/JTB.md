# Journal of Theoretical Biology (JTB)

Peer-reviewed journal publishing theoretical approaches to biological problems, including mathematical and conceptual models of evolution, ecology, and behaviour. Published by Elsevier.

---

## 1975

- [Zahavi (1975) *JTB*](../references/Zahavi_1975_JTB.md) — "Mate Selection—A Selection for a Handicap" — the founding statement of the handicap principle; argues characters developed through mate preference confer survival handicaps on the selected sex, and that these handicaps are useful to the selecting sex precisely because they test mate quality; the handicap requires no genetic linkage between marker and quality and thereby excludes bluff; offered as an individual-level alternative to group- and kin-selection accounts of exaggerated ornament, territory size, and "altruistic" plumage
