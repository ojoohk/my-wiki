# Ritual — Collective Ritual, Ritual Evolution, Ritual Signaling

Research on the form, function, and evolution of ritual behavior, with emphasis on collective ritual as a cross-cultural universal. Includes evolutionary accounts, cognitive and neuroscientific perspectives, and empirical studies of ritual's social and psychological consequences.

## Core Concepts

- **Collective ritual**: Following Rappaport (1999), the performance of more or less invariant sequences of formal acts and utterances not entirely encoded by the performers; non-instrumental, conventional, and performative.
- **Cooperative signaling platform**: The hypothesis (Lang & Kundt, 2023) that the defining function of collective ritual is to communicate cooperative intentions via multiple redundant, degenerate, and pluripotent signals.
- **Similarity signals**: Ritualized mimicry or synchronous movement that indicates shared interests / mutual relatedness between signaler and receiver.
- **Coalitional signals**: Ritualized gestures or markings that indicate membership in a specific cooperative ensemble or joint action.
- **Commitment signals**: Costly self-inflicted behaviors (pain, resource sacrifice) that honestly signal the graded strength of obligation to a risky joint action; grounded in costly signaling theory.
- **Complex signaling system**: A set of signals that function as a unit and are redundant, degenerate, and pluripotent (Barker et al., 2019; Hebets et al., 2016).
- **Ontogenetic ritualization**: Process by which a dyad shortens an idiosyncratic string of gestures into a simple placeholder gesture through repeated interaction (Tomasello et al., 1997).

## 핵심 개념

- **집합적 의례**: Rappaport(1999)의 정의에 따라, 수행자가 완전히 규정하지 않은 형식화된 행위와 발화의 불변 연쇄를 수행하는 것; 비도구적, 관습적, 수행적 특성을 가짐.
- **협력적 신호 플랫폼**: 집합적 의례의 핵심 기능이 다중의 잉여적·축퇴적·다능적 신호를 통해 협력 의도를 전달하는 것이라는 가설 (Lang & Kundt, 2023).
- **유사성 신호**: 신호자와 수신자 사이의 공유 이해관계/상호 유사성을 나타내는 의례화된 모방 또는 동기화된 움직임.
- **연합 신호**: 특정 협력 집단이나 공동 행동 참여 의사를 나타내는 의례화된 몸짓 또는 표식.
- **헌신 신호**: 위험한 공동 행동에 대한 헌신 강도를 정직하게 신호하는 고비용 행동(고통, 자원 낭비); 비용 신호 이론에 근거.
